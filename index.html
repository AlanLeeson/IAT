Arab/Muslim Implicit Association Test

All materials obtained from Project Implicit. To view more information, visit their website at https://implicit.harvard.edu/implicit/ Xu, K., Lofaro, N., Nosek, B. A., & Greenwald, A. G. (2016, January 7). Arab IAT 2004-2015. Retrieved from osf.io/t8u7p

The words at the top represent two categories: Arab/Muslim or Other people. Names will appear in the center of the screen. Press the button on the bottom that corresponds to the category you believe is correct. An X appears if you have made an error; correct it to proceed. Make sure you go as fast as you can.
